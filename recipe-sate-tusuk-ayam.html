Sate Tusuk Ayam (Sate Chicken Skewers)

Sate is one of Indonesiaâ€™s favourite street food. You can often find locals setting up shop on random street corners or steps grilling up sates for dirt cheap. It was one of my favourite mid-day snacks while wandering markets and random streets.

Ingredients

  • 500 gr chicken breast

Marinate

  • 3 tbsp shallots chopped
  • 2 tbsp garlic chopped
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 5 tbsp coconut oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice chicken into thick cubes then marinate in sauce.
  2. Thread the chicken onto wooden or stainless-steel skewers. (Note: If using wooden skewers, remember to soak them in water for 30 minutes before threading chicken onto them)
  3. Grill chicken until lightly browned. As you grill baste the skewers with leftover marinade.
  4. Serve with peanut sauce (saus kacang)
